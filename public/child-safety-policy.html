Child Safety Policy

Effective Date: 2025-07-22

Introduction

Sigor World ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to ensuring the safety of children and protecting them from exploitation, abuse, and harm. This Child Safety Policy outlines our stance, measures, and procedures to prevent and address child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and other inappropriate content involving minors.

We strictly prohibit the use of our services to upload, distribute, or promote CSAM or any content that exploits or endangers children.

Our Commitment

  • We do not allow any content on our platform that depicts, promotes, or encourages child sexual exploitation or abuse.
  • We immediately remove any content reported or detected that violates this policy.
  • We cooperate with law enforcement and relevant authorities when necessary.

Reporting Violations

If you encounter any content on our platform that you believe involves the exploitation or abuse of a minor, please report it immediately by contacting us at:

Email: sigorworldofficial@gmail.com

We will promptly investigate and take appropriate action, including removing the content and notifying the proper authorities if required.

Monitoring and Enforcement

  • We use both manual and technical measures to monitor for violations of this policy.
  • Accounts found to be engaging in CSAM-related activities are terminated without notice, and evidence may be shared with law enforcement agencies.

Education and Awareness

We encourage all our users to be vigilant and help us maintain a safe environment by reporting any suspicious or harmful behavior.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Child Safety Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page along with the updated effective date.
